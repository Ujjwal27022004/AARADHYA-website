RESPAWN

Overview

Respawn is an e-sports event specially conducted for enthusiastic gamers. The games included are PUBG Mobile, Counter Strike and FIFA 19 split across different gaming platforms like Mobile Gaming, PC Gaming and Play station respectively. Any gamer with no age barrier can participate in this event.

Gaming is becoming a career path today and certainly the players who play these so-called videogames are turning their heads to make it as a career option, this was very well understood by Adhyaaya and respawn is brought to cater this.
